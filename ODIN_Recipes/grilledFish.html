The Perfect Grilled Fish Recipe

Perfectly sizzled grilled fish!

Have ever wondered how to make the succulent and delicious African grilled fish? Here is your chance to do it yourself:

Ingredients
  • Wild caught talapia
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Thyme
  • Basil
  • Kosher salt
  • Ginger
Steps
  1. Chop onions and other Ingredients
  2. Blend together finely
  3. Rub kosher salt on the fish
  4. Rub in blended ingredients and marinate for 2 hours
  5. Bake at 350 degrees till golden brown
